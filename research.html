Research

We decode receptor signaling logic and build tools to precisely modulate pathways in cells, organoids, and in vivo — with a focus on Wnt/Frizzled, regenerative biology, and translation.

1) Wnt/Frizzled signaling

Receptor complex assembly, signalosome dynamics, and β-catenin tuning.

FZDLRP5/6Dishevelledβ-catenin
What we’re asking
  • How do multivalent ligands drive FZD–LRP6 clustering and bias outputs?
  • Where are the rate-limiting steps for pathway activation in different tissues?
  • How does crosstalk rewire signaling in disease contexts?
Related publicationsTeam

2) Therapeutic protein design

Engineered antibodies & Wnt surrogates to selectively engage receptors.

Bi-specificsAvidityStability
What we’re building
  • Surrogates that mimic Wnt with receptor and valency control.
  • Antibodies that bias signaling for tissue repair.
  • Delivery and half-life optimization for in vivo efficacy.
Related publicationsTeam

3) Functional genomics

Loss- and gain-of-function screens to map control points & vulnerabilities.

  • Genome-scale screens to chart Wnt pathway regulators.
  • State-specific dependencies in disease and regeneration.
  • Network integration with proteomics for mechanism.
