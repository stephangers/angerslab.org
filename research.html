Research

Three themes spanning receptor logic, regeneration, and cancer functional genomics.

We study how Wnt/Frizzled signaling controls stem cell behavior and build receptor-specific Wnt agonists to precisely modulate those circuits in vivo. In parallel, we run genome-scale CRISPR programs in glioblastoma stem cells.
